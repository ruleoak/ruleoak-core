# Public RuleOak Core / Private SafeDesk Strategy

RuleOak should use two product surfaces.

## Public surface

The public surface exists to earn developer trust and create adoption:

- RuleOak Core v2.2.0 under AGPL-3.0-or-later plus commercial license option.
- RuleOak Protocol under MIT for schemas, evidence fixtures, badge specs, and conformance examples.
- selected adapters and selected safe demos.
- public documentation, examples, synthetic fixtures, and release notes.

## Private/commercial surface

The private surface is where consumer product value and monetization live:

- RuleOak SafeDesk full app.
- polished desktop UI and future mobile UI.
- installer/signing scripts.
- premium report templates.
- paid feature logic.
- customer/license logic.
- commercial backup/restore and support flows.
- full vertical workflows for Home Evidence, Creator Proof, Travel Proof, and Freelancer Proof.

## Principle

Open enough of the system to prove the engine and build trust. Keep the packaged consumer product private until traction and pricing are validated.
